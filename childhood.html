Time Travel

Childhood Memories

Throughout my childhood, Dad had a cine camera recording every outing.

The excitement of going to the cinema to see the latest Beatles film HELP.

Visiting an aunt in her brand new high rise flat, embracing the modern era.

Enjoying the local outdoor swimming pool

And most of all recording simple family fun in the garden

This included an array of pets, from tortoise to Henry Gbson, the guinea pig. And endless kittens.

Thes footage would be viewed on a large plastic screen in a cramped living room. Especially by visitors.

Out would come, the imaginatively sliced, reels of the latest escapades. Everybody loved it.

Over the years those memories have been treasured. I always knew my beloved Dads legacy still existed.

Last year, a much anticipated package arrived in the post.

The art of Cine to DVD technology meant that on my telly I could travel back to my childhood.

It was just as wonderful as I remember.

    ABOUT TIME is a 2013 film written by Richard Curtis.

    Like every romantic comedy it has a tempting tag line.

    What if every moment in life came with a 2nd chance...

    A poignant look at life, love and family.

    The cast included Bill Nighy and Domhnall Gleeson.
