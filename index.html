Let's talk about Time Travel

Over 100 years ago Albert Einstein published the Theory of Special Relativity.

This started the Time Travel debate including hairy wormholes & quantum foam.

Scientists have been politely disagreeing about it ever since.

The concept of 'moving through different points of time' has been around for centuries

Writers saw the endless possibilities and Science Fiction was born.

Today the spectrum of Sci-Fi includes film-making, TV, video games, fandoms and much more.

It is a massive money making industry and fans can't get enough of it.

And rightly so, this form of escapism can be everything we want it to be.

Where would we be without it?
